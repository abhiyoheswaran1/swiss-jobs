Swiss Job Market & AI Exposure

Visualizing 0 Swiss jobs across 0 occupations from the Swiss Federal Statistical Office (BFS) SAKE survey and wage data. Each rectangle is an occupation — size is number of jobs, color is the selected metric. This is not a report or economic forecast — it is an exploratory tool for visualising Swiss labour data.

Methodology & Data Sources

Employment: BFS Swiss Labour Force Survey (SAKE) 2019–2021 cumulated, CH-ISCO-19 3-digit level. Wages: BFS Wage Structure Survey (LSE) 2022, median gross monthly wages by ISCO-08 2-digit group. AI Exposure: Each occupation scored 0–10 by Claude (Anthropic) following Andrej Karpathy's methodology — work done entirely on computers faces the highest exposure. Scores do NOT predict job disappearance; they indicate the degree of AI-driven transformation.
