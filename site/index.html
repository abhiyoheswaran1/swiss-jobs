Methodology & Data Sources

Employment data: Eurostat Labour Force Survey (lfsa_egai2d), 2023, Switzerland, ISCO-08 two-digit level. Wage data: BFS Wage Structure Survey (LSE) 2022, median gross monthly wages by occupation group. AI Exposure scores follow Andrej Karpathy's methodology: a 0-10 scale measuring how fundamentally AI will reshape each occupation over the next 5-10 years. Key principle: work done entirely on computers faces the highest exposure. Scores do NOT predict job disappearance. Education levels are mapped from ISCO-08 skill level requirements.
